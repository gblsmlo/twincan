## Why Twincan?

Most boilerplates are bloated or outdated. **Twincan** is opinionated and built for speed.

We replaced the slow parts (ESLint/Prettier -> **Biome**) and the complex parts (Legacy Auth -> **Better Auth**) so you can focus on building your product, not your infrastructure.

### The "Bleeding Edge" Stack
-   **Performance First:** Built on **Next.js 16** (App Router) and styled with **Tailwind v4**.
-   **Type-Safe Backend:** **Drizzle ORM** + **PostgreSQL** ensures your data layer is robust.
-   **Next-Gen Auth:** Integrated with **Better Auth** for secure, hassle-free authentication.
-   **Scalable Architecture:** Modular design (`src/modules`) that grows with your MRR.
